Bio

Donovan is a front-end developer based in Los Angeles, CA. Born in Baltimore, Donovan relocated to the west coast to pursue one of his passions of producing music.

After working as a music producer for over 10 years, Donovan saw how intertwined the worlds of music and technology have become. This sparked a curiosity and an interest in gaining the skills to be able to contribute to the growing technological revolution. That's where Kenzie Academy came in.

As a part of Kenzie Academy's Spring '22 Web Development cohort, Donovan has gained proficiency in web app building essentials (HTML5, CSS, and JavaScript). Currently, his goal is to work on challenging projects and continue to grow as a developer.
