Bio

My name is Donovan, and I'm a junior Front-End Developer based in Los Angeles, CA. Born and raised in Baltimore, I relocated to the west coast in 2013 to pursue my passion of producing music.

After working as a music producer for over 10 years, I realized how intertwined the worlds of music and technology have become. This sparked a curiosity and an interest in gaining the skills to be able to contribute to the growing technological revolution. That's where Kenzie Academy came in.

As a part of Kenzie Academy's Spring '22 Web Development cohort, I have gained proficiency in web application-building essentials (HTML5, CSS, and JavaScript). Currently, my goal is to work on challenging projects and continue to grow as a developer. - Donovan
